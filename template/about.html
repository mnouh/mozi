Mozi was created in December of 2012 by Chris Paquette and Mohamed Nouh while both were students at the State University of New York at Binghamton. Understanding the need for a simple, no-hassle payment system, the duo developed Mozi with the aims to create a low-cost solution that could be integrated with social media. The vision for Mozi goes beyond the P2P payment we enjoy today--the two are busily at work creating new payment solutions that they hope will redefine how money is transfered in all situations.
