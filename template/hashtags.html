Hashtag #Commands

Hashtag commands are designed to simply communicate information between Mozi users and businesses via Twitter. We've designed them to give you full control over your Mozi experience so you can quickly execute orders, share experiences, and get recommendations. Remember, at anytime you can tweet @myMozi with the hashtag #commands to get a current list of commands. Mozi users can also submit requests for new commands here. Enjoy!

#buy

Quickly buy something you see on social media

The #buy command initiates a rapid order/purchase request so you can buy items that are mentioned in business tweets (by replying "@business #buy"). A payment confirmation tweet will be sent to you where you will be required to reply with #pay to authorize the transaction. Orders are automatically sent to your #home address.

#pay

Confirm and authorize transactions to your account

Mozi requires users to authorize payments by responding to tweets with #pay. Confirmation tweets are sent from @myMozi and take the form of @username BUSINESS is requesting payment of AMOUNT. Respond with #pay to confirm. By replying with #pay, the order will be processed and your primary card will be charged.

#order

Easily customize orders to your specifications

The #order command is similar to #buy except it is used for processing purchases that do not have set #hashtags or for orders that need user input like food or drink orders at restaurants, delivery orders, or when otherwise specified. Command #order records the text within your tweet so that you can communicate order specifications (i.e. cooking temperature) directly to the business.

+ #order #home

The combination of these two will request an order to be delivered to your #home location as set in your Mozi Interface

+ #order #work

The combination of these two will request an order to be delivered to your #work location as set in your Mozi Interface

#share

Add #share to your orders to share your purchase with your friends

Including #share on any transaction will automatically publish a story to your Mozi Stream and Twitter Feed (users can choose where to share through Account Settings). You can attach pictures and type text to be included in your shared experience.

#recommend

Get automatic friend recommendations at Mozi businesses

Mozi gives you the ability to see what your friends have purchased at businesses so you can get ideas for what to buy. We quickly tally all the transactions your followers have purchased at the business and then recommend for you those that were most frequently purchased.

#menu

Get access to restaurant menus instantly

Unsure of what to order from a Mozi restaurant? By tweeting @BUSINESS #menu, Mozi will send you a link to the business menu so you can instantly see what's offered at that business.
